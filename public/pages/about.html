Mission And Vision

N.E.M is an Evangelical Movement, devoted to the prophetic works of our Lord Jesus Christ being made manifest as it was of old. The Salvation of lost soul, through her evangelistic, prophetic meetings and crusades. Also to build bridges between different denominations, bringing hope to the hopeless, and help to the helpless. In the past eight (8) years the ministry has ran crusades and family deliverances in different parts of the country. N.E.M's mission is to propagate the gospel of Jesus Christ to the uttermost parts of the world, in a bid to depopulate hell and populate heaven. (Matthew 28:18-20)

Nkechi Evangelical Ministry

Nkechi Evangelical Ministry (also known as Zion City God's Dwelling Place) is an inter-denominational ministry dedicated to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ. The ministry began on the 24th of August 2017 at Obegu Town, Ugwunagbo L.G.A of Abia State, with 560 people in attendance. On the 7th of November 2017, the ministry’s headquarters, located at No. 1 Arise and Shine Bus Stop, Akpajo East West Road, Rivers State, was officially commissioned.

Prophetess Nkechi Favour Okata, the founder of the ministry, is a spiritual counsellor, prophetess by calling, preacher, songwriter, and public speaker. She was born on the 14th of August 1981 in Obegu Town, Abia State. On her 35th birthday, she experienced a divine encounter with the Lord:

She heard His audible voice saying, “Go and tell the world that Jesus saves” (Luke 4:18-19)

The Lord empowered her by placing fire in her right palm, saying, “Go, root out and pull down, destroy, throw down, build and plant” (Jeremiah 1:10).

From this encounter, Nkechi Evangelical Ministry was birthed, established, and mandated by God to proclaim salvation, healing, deliverance, and restoration.

Prophetess Nkechi Favour Okata has also expressed her calling through gospel music. She has released an album titled “Favour Volume One”, featuring spirit-filled songs of worship, praise, and encouragement that uplift the body of Christ and inspire believers to draw closer to God.
